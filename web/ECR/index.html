Welcome to the official website of the 2015 Canadian Eastern Regional (ECR) Return Top Championship. As sanctioned by the Canadian Yoyo Association (CANYA), the top 3 places of each return top division who live in Ontario or Quebec will be seeded to the finals of the Canadian National Yo-yo Contest in 2016. Foreigners (including non-Canadians) who do not live in Ontario or Quebec may also compete for prizes, but will not be eligible for the seeds.

This family-friendly event is open to the public. Admission for spectators is FREE. Competitors are required to register with a small fee.

Updates

Join our Facebook event for the latest announcements!

  • Full results for Open, 1A preliminaries and finals are available. Congratulations to Benson Fok, Alexis JV, and Anson Liang for earning seeds to the 1A finals of Canadian Nationals 2016, which will take place in the Ontario Science Center in Toronto on March 26, 2016
  • New rule for return top open division: Only ONE of 2A, 3A, 4A, or 5A is allowed. No combining multiple styles.
  • There will be no intermediate kendama battle ladders.

Divisions

  • Return top 1A
  • Return top open
  • Return tor sport ladder
  • Kendama beginner battle ladder
  • Kendama mini games

If you'd like to compete in any of the above divisions, please register by November 18th!
