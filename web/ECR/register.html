Music upload instructions (Return top 1A and Open only)

If you are only competing in kendama or return sport ladder divisions, you may skip this step and proceed to submit.

  • Preliminary is 1 minute, and final is 2 minutes. There will not be preliminaries for Open divison.
  • If your music is longer than the allotted time, your music will be stopped at either 1:00 or 2:00 of the music file.
  • You must have all music ready and selected before submitting. If you are not competing in one of the divisions, you can leave the field(s) blank.
  • If you want to use the same music for more than one freestyle, please select the same file again. Do not leave the fields blank.
  • Vocals containing offensive slurs or obscenities in any language will result in disqualification. If you are not sure, please email contest organizers directly before uploading.
  • Only .mp3 and .wav files are accepted. Please keep the file size smaller than 10 MB for each file!
